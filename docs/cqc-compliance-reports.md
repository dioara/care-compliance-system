# CQC Compliance Reports Research

## Overview
The Care Quality Commission (CQC) is the independent regulator of health and social care in England. Care homes and domiciliary care services must comply with fundamental standards and produce various compliance reports.

## Fundamental Standards (14 Core Standards)
1. **Person-centred care** - Tailored care meeting individual needs
2. **Visiting and accompanying** - Visitor rights and accompaniment
3. **Dignity and respect** - Privacy, equality, independence
4. **Consent** - Informed consent before treatment
5. **Safety** - Risk assessment and qualified staff
6. **Safeguarding from abuse** - Protection from neglect and harm
7. **Food and drink** - Adequate nutrition
8. **Premises and equipment** - Clean, suitable facilities
9. **Complaints** - Effective complaints handling
10. **Good governance** - Quality and safety systems
11. **Staffing** - Adequate qualified staff
12. **Fit and proper staff** - Recruitment checks
13. **Duty of candour** - Transparency about incidents
14. **Display of ratings** - Public display of CQC ratings

## Required Compliance Reports

### 1. **Audit Compliance Report**
- Overall compliance percentage across all 14 standards
- Breakdown by each fundamental standard
- RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status for each section
- Non-compliant items with action plans
- Timeline: Monthly/Quarterly

### 2. **Safeguarding Report**
- Incidents of abuse or neglect
- Safeguarding referrals made
- Training completion rates
- Policies and procedures review
- Timeline: Monthly

### 3. **Staffing Report**
- Staff-to-resident ratios
- Qualification levels and training records
- DBS check status
- Supervision and appraisal completion
- Recruitment checks compliance
- Timeline: Monthly

### 4. **Incident and Accident Report**
- All incidents and accidents logged
- Root cause analysis
- Actions taken and lessons learned
- Trends and patterns
- Serious incidents reported to CQC
- Timeline: Monthly

### 5. **Medication Management Report**
- Medication errors
- Audit results
- Staff competency assessments
- Storage and administration compliance
- Timeline: Monthly

### 6. **Health and Safety Report**
- Fire safety checks
- Equipment maintenance
- Risk assessments
- Environmental audits
- COSHH compliance
- Timeline: Monthly

### 7. **Quality Assurance Report**
- Service user feedback and satisfaction
- Complaints and compliments
- Quality audits completed
- Action plans progress
- Timeline: Quarterly

### 8. **Training Compliance Report**
- Mandatory training completion rates
- Specialist training (dementia, mental health, etc.)
- Induction completion
- Refresher training due dates
- Timeline: Monthly

### 9. **Care Planning and Review Report**
- Care plan reviews completed on time
- Person-centred care evidence
- Family involvement
- Goal achievement
- Timeline: Monthly

### 10. **Governance and Leadership Report**
- Management oversight activities
- Policy reviews
- Business continuity planning
- Financial sustainability
- Provider visits and audits
- Timeline: Quarterly

### 11. **Infection Prevention and Control Report**
- IPC audits
- Outbreak management
- Hand hygiene compliance
- PPE stock and usage
- Cleaning schedules
- Timeline: Monthly

### 12. **Nutrition and Hydration Report**
- Nutritional assessments
- Weight monitoring
- Special diet compliance
- Food safety audits
- Hydration monitoring
- Timeline: Monthly

## Report Format Requirements
- Executive summary
- Data visualization (charts, graphs)
- Trend analysis
- Action plans for non-compliance
- Responsible persons and deadlines
- Evidence and supporting documentation
- Sign-off by registered manager

## CQC Inspection Preparation
- All reports should be readily available
- Evidence of continuous improvement
- Clear audit trails
- Staff awareness of compliance status
- Action plans with progress tracking

## Data Sources for Reports
- Audit results from compliance audits
- Incident logs
- Staff records
- Training databases
- Care plans
- Medication administration records (MAR)
- Maintenance logs
- Feedback and complaints
- Quality assurance checks
