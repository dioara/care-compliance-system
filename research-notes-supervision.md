# Staff Supervision and Appraisal Audit - Research Notes

## CQC Regulation 18: Staffing Requirements

### Key Requirements for Supervision and Appraisal:

**Regulation 18(2)(a)**: Staff must receive appropriate support, training, professional development, supervision and appraisal as necessary to enable them to carry out their duties.

### Supervision Requirements:
1. **Initial Supervision**: Staff must be supervised until they can demonstrate required/acceptable levels of competence
2. **Ongoing Supervision**: Staff should receive appropriate ongoing or periodic supervision to ensure competence is maintained
3. **Clinical/Professional Supervision**: Health, social and other care professionals must have access to clinical or professional supervision as required by their professional regulator
4. **Frequency**: While CQC doesn't mandate specific frequency, industry standard is:
   - Every 6-8 weeks for care staff
   - Every 8-12 weeks for senior staff
   - More frequent for new staff or those requiring additional support

### Appraisal Requirements:
1. **Regular Appraisals**: Staff should receive regular appraisal of their performance from an appropriately skilled and experienced person
2. **Training Needs Identification**: Appraisals should identify training, learning and development needs
3. **Planning and Support**: Identified needs should be planned for and supported
4. **Annual Frequency**: Industry standard is annual appraisals with mid-year reviews

### Documentation Requirements:
1. Supervision records showing:
   - Date and duration
   - Topics discussed
   - Actions agreed
   - Staff member and supervisor signatures
2. Appraisal records showing:
   - Performance review
   - Training needs identified
   - Development goals
   - Action plans
3. Training matrix showing completion of identified training
4. Competency assessments

### Audit Focus Areas:
1. **Frequency and Consistency**: Are supervisions happening at required intervals?
2. **Quality of Supervision**: Are discussions meaningful and documented?
3. **Action Follow-up**: Are agreed actions completed?
4. **Appraisal Completion**: Are all staff receiving annual appraisals?
5. **Training Needs**: Are identified needs being met?
6. **Competency Maintenance**: Is ongoing competence being assessed?
7. **Professional Development**: Are staff supported to develop?
8. **Record Quality**: Are records complete, accurate, and accessible?
